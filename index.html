About Aspire

The 6th edition of ASPIRE, the Annual Social Entrepreneurship Conclave organized by ENACTUS NSUT is aimed at being a unique platform for young entrepreneurs to meet each other and interact with brilliant corporate and business minds. It is a one-of-a-kind entrepreneurship convention, which invites budding innovators in the social sector who want to make a positive change in the society through entrepreneurial action. With a footfall of 1000+ during a period of 2 days of the convention, this year, with your support we are determined to make it even bigger and better. Last year, ASPIRE got participation from over 40 colleges in Delhi (IITD, DTU, St. Stephens, LSR, SSCBS, SRCC to name a few) and more colleges are expected to participate this year.
